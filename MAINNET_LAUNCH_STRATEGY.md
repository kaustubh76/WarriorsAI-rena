## 11. Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Smart contract bug | Medium | Critical | Audit, emergency pause, bug bounty |
| AI API downtime | Medium | High | Fallback resolution, queue system |
| 0G network issues | Low | High | Multiple RPC endpoints, graceful degradation |
| Frontend crash | Medium | Medium | Error boundaries, monitoring, CDN |

### Business Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Low initial adoption | Medium | High | Seed battles, incentive programs |
| Negative community sentiment | Low | High | Rapid response team, transparent comms |
| Competitor launch | Medium | Medium | Focus on unique AI angle |
| Regulatory concerns | Low | High | Legal review, geo-restrictions if needed |

### Operational Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Team burnout | High | High | Sustainable schedule, clear roles |
| Support overwhelm | Medium | Medium | FAQ, bot automation, community mods |
| Content fatigue | Medium | Low | Content calendar, batch creation |

### Emergency Response Plan

```
SEVERITY 1 (Critical - Funds at risk):
1. Pause contracts immediately
2. Post acknowledgment within 15 min
3. War room until resolved
4. Post-mortem within 24 hours

SEVERITY 2 (Major - Feature broken):
1. Acknowledge within 1 hour
2. Workaround if possible
3. Fix within 24 hours
4. Update community

SEVERITY 3 (Minor - UX issue):
1. Log and prioritize
2. Fix in next release
3. No immediate action needed
```
